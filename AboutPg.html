About Us

Our goal is to calculate the highest wind speeds and trends in cities to predict wind speeds for the next week. We created this websites to inform residents in urban cities all over the United States and Canada of future damaging wind speeds in the selected city.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inform the public and make them aware of future dangerous wind speeds in their areas.
